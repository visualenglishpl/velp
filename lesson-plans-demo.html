Lesson Plans

Beginner Lesson Plan

45 minutes

Objectives:

  • Learn basic greetings and introductions
  • Practice pronunciation of key phrases
  • Begin building classroom vocabulary

Lesson Steps:

  • Warm-up: Show flashcards with classroom objects and review names

    (5 minutes)

  • Introduction: Introduce greetings using puppet dialogue

    (10 minutes)

  • Practice: Group activity with greeting practice

    (15 minutes)

  • Activity: Hello song with actions

    (10 minutes)

Materials Needed:

Flashcards, puppets, Hello song recording, activity sheets

Advanced Lesson Plan

45 minutes

Objectives:

  • Review and expand greeting vocabulary
  • Practice dialogue-based communication
  • Apply greetings in role-play scenarios

Lesson Steps:

  • Review: Quick review of previously learned phrases

    (5 minutes)

  • Vocabulary: Introduce formal vs. informal greetings

    (10 minutes)

  • Dialogue: Small group role-play with dialogue cards

    (15 minutes)

  • Game: Greeting chain game

    (10 minutes)

Materials Needed:

Dialogue cards, greeting flashcards, game props, workbook pages
